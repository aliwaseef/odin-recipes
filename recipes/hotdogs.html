Easy And Quick Breakfast Sandwiches

Easy to make hotdogs

  • 1/2 medium ripe avocado, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 6 hotdogs
  • 6 hotdog buns, split
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped red onion
  • 3 beef bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
  1. In a small bowl, mash avocado with a fork, stirring in lime juice, salt and pepper.
  2. Grill hot dogs, covered, over medium heat until heated through, 7-9 minutes, turning occasionally
  3. Serve in buns. Top with avocado mixture, tomato, onion and beef bacon
